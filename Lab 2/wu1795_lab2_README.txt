Electrical Connections

	Potentiometer
		Pin 1 connected to the 3V pin of the ESP32
		Pin 2 connected to pin 34, A2 pin for the ADC 1 connection
		Pin 3 connected to the GND pin of the ESP32

	LED
		Longer pin (+ pin) connected to pin 8, TX pin for the PWM connection
		Shorter pin (- pin) connected to a 220 Ohm resistor

	Resistor
		One side connected to shorter pin of LED (- pin)
		Other side connected to GND pin of the ESP32
